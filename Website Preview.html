Get paid

Your Stripe account. Your money. Your timing.

FirmLock is the only platform in its category that uses Stripe Connect Standard — meaning you connect your own Stripe account and funds flow directly to you.

  • Two-minute setup. Link your existing Stripe account or create a new one in the flow.
  • Funds never touch us. Payments go from client card to your Stripe balance. Full stop.
  • ACH, card, or wire. Let clients pay the way that works for them.
  • Automatic follow-up. Polite, professional invoice reminders that sound like you wrote them.
